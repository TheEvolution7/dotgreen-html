Introduce

Thank you for the companionship of all athletes and partners of Trang An Marathon during the past 2 seasons.

Trang An Marathon season 3 will be officially organized by Ninh Binh Provincial People's Committee in collaboration with Vu Media Joint Stock Company (VRace), promising to bring athletes an exciting season and unforgettable memories when visiting here..

WITH TRANG AN MARATHON 2023 CREATING FOOTSTRIKE FROM THE HERITAGE

Trang An is always likened to "Ha Long on land" with the magnificent beauty of nature. From majestic mountain ranges, surrounded by clear water, mysterious caves to idyllic beautiful streamside rice fields. All create a dreamy, beautiful picture frame that gradually opens up in front of our eyes.

Trang An Marathon 2023 hopes that the runners will not only run on the beautiful route with our race, but those legs will also roam, releasing their souls with the sun and wind, creating long runs in the distance heritage when coming to Trang An Cultural and Natural Heritage Complex.

Let's visit Trang An and admire the beauty here!

Regulations & Charter

These rules and regulations are designed to help organize and manage the event on a large scale, ensuring the safety of event attendees in compliance with the provisions of the law of the Vietnam Athletics Federation. Nam about the recognition of participants' achievements and the responsibility of the event.

I. Conditions of participation

1. Participants

- Athletes voluntarily participating in Trang An Marathon 2023 have read, understood and agreed to the information, documents and competition rules set by the Organizer.

- Athletes are Vietnamese citizens and foreigners who fully meet the provisions of the Race’s Regulations, not during the period of being banned by the National and International Federation of Associations or the disciplinary management agency.

- Athletes participating the distance of 21km must be 16 or older and complete the registration with the Organizer.

- Athletes participating the distance of 42km must be 18 or older and complete the registration with the Organizer.

- Athletes under the age of 18 must have a responsible guardian and sign a "disclaimer".

- Athletes under the age of 12 must have a parent or guardian to monitor and take responsibility for the duration of the race.

- Athletes understand that participating in Trang An Marathon 2023 is an activity that requires good physical strength and the athletes have to practice and prepare for the necessary health. The organizers will not be responsible if the athletes are physically, mentally injured during the race

2.Registration

- Online: https://ticket.irace.vn/trang-an-marathon-2023

VuMedia Joint Stock Company. No. 10A1, lane 376, Khuong Dinh street, Ha Dinh, Thanh Xuan, Hanoi.

- After the registration is properly processed, the Organizer will not refund the registration fee to the athletes for any reason.

- The event participation fee will not be refunded in case the event is canceled due to force majeure (earthquake, flood, fire, war, strike, national mourning...)

3. Regulations

- For athletes of the provincial, city, branch, and federation teams: according to the form stamped with the certification of the governing body about their personnel issues.

- For other athletes: Register according to the website: https://actiup.net/event/trang-an-marathon-2023  

- The time to complete the profile and the registration are informed on the Website.

- Quantity: Expected 5,000 athletes.

II. Time, place of the race

1. Place (Expected)

Location: Hoa Lu ancient capital, Truong Yen, Hoa Lu, Ninh Binh, Viet Nam

2. (Expected)

November 11th 2023, at 8am - 7pm

+ 3pm: Opening ceremony

+ Race Kit Pick-up Area opened

+ Booth activities provide information about the event

+ Other event activities

November 12th, 2023

+ 04:30 am: Athletes gather

+ 05:30 am: 42km Athletes start 

+ 06:00 am: 21km Athletes start 

+ 06:30 am: 10km Athletes start 

+ 07:00 am: 5km Athletes start 

+ 10:00 am: Closing Ceremony 

+ 12:00 am: Course closed

- Participants: Professional athletes, movement athletes and people in Hoa Lu district, Ninh Binh province.

- Cut-off time: The athletes must be able to complete the race, from the start line to the finish line, within the time period below:

+ 42km: maximum completion time is 6 hours 30 minutes.

+ 21km: maximum completion time is 3 hours 30 minutes.

+ 10km: maximum completion time is 2 hours.

+ 5km: maximum completion time is 1 hour.

- Athletes must give priority to medical staff and ambulances throughout the race regardless of their position on the track.

- During the race, all runners are expected to behave in a professional and courteous manner. Do not use profanity, quarrel, insult, have unsportsmanlike behavior towards other athletes. Anyone who violates will be disqualified.

- All acts of littering, environmental damage, etc. are strictly prohibited. The athletes are responsible for protecting the environment around the race.

- Athletes must wear a shirt (including the BIB number) during the competition. It must be clear at all times.

III. Prize and other regulations

1. Prize: (updated later)

2. Tthe track

- Athletes who leave the track will not be allowed to return for any purpose. Athletes who intentionally shorten track will be disqualified. The fraudulent acts are evidenced by the referee, members of the Organizer.

- Athletes of the 42km, 21km, 10km, and 5km distances who participate in the race without the official BIB number, a chip time or a chip time that is not their own will be disqualified.

- Athletes must not receive any assistance by means of wheeled or motorized vehicles during the competition. Except for disabled athletes with the permission of the Organizer.

- Athletes are not allowed to bring animals, explosives, flammable substances, compressed air, toxic substances and anything that can cause danger or inconvenience to others during the race

3. Competition results

- Competition results will be recorded by a timekeeping device mounted on the BIB. Beginning when the athletes crosses the start line and stops when crosses the finish line.

- An athlete may not be recognized as a finisher if:

+ Wearing more than 2 timekeeping devices.

+ The athlete's completion time exceeds the cut-off time.

+ Abnormal absences on the track confirmed by the referee and recording equipment evidence.

- Official results will be announced by the Organizer on the website. Athletes have the right to complain, request verification of their own or other athletes' results, along with clear evidence. Complaint requests should be sent before November 21, 2023.

- Prizes will be officially announced on the day of the competition. All complaints about the results of First, Second and Third prizes with all distances must be made before 09:00 am on November 12, 2023

- Athletes are responsible for complying with the regulations and instructions of the Organizer on the track to ensure accurate results.

- The decision of the Organizer is final

4. Discipline:

Violators will be handled according to Decree 158/2013/ND-CP dated  November 12, 2013 of the Government on sanctioning of administrative violations in the fields of culture, sports, tourism and advertising.

5. Force majeure

- The Organizer is not responsible when the race is canceled or postponed due to force majeure.

- The Organizer has the right to make changes to the rules and regulations of the race and will be notified to the athletes in writing, email or at the scene before the race takes place.

- The Organizer has the full right to use the information provided by the participants and the images, videos of the race for legal purposes in advertising, exhibitions... to promote the race

Each athlete is responsible for understanding and complying with the rules of the race, violation will be disqualified

Only the Organizer has the right to update the Charter if necessary. For tenors not included in the charter, the final decision belongs to the Organizer.
